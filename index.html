About Rick Sanchez

Rick Sanchez is a genius scientist and the grandfather of Morty Smith, with whom he goes on adventures through multiple dimensions. He is known for his intelligence, sarcasm, and his disregard for the well-being of those around him, including his family. Despite his flaws, he cares deeply for his daughter and granddaughter.

Achievements

  • Invented a portal gun that can transport him and Morty to different dimensions
  • Created a formula for inter-dimensional cable, which allows him to access unlimited TV channels from other dimensions
  • Developed a love potion that temporarily made the entire world fall in love with him
  • Found a cure for the Cromulons' powerful telepathic control over the population of Earth

Adventures

  • Visited a world ruled by balls of gas with advanced technology
  • Helped a hive-minded species to unite their queen with her king
  • Saved an entire dimension from being destroyed by a giant head
  • Survived an attack from the Galactic Federation and its president, Tammy
